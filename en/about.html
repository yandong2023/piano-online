About Piano Online

Bring music a little closer to everyone.

Piano Online is a piano that lives in the browser. We want to reduce the barriers of equipment, space and complicated first lessons, so you can begin when the desire to play appears.

Start playingExplore songs
Our direction

Help people make music first, then understand the piano.

Many people do not dislike piano. The first step is simply too heavy: expensive equipment, dense lessons and slow feedback. We begin with a lighter doorway—open a page, press a key and hear a note immediately.

When someone wants to continue, guided songs, rhythm practice and beginner lessons can gradually build an understanding of keys, timing and melody.

“Music should not wait until every condition is perfect before you are allowed to begin.”
Product principles

Three things we want to keep true

01

Ready immediately

No required signup or download. The first note should happen within seconds.

02

Learn through real songs

Place practice inside familiar melodies so every step produces a musical result.

03

Promise only what exists

Today we focus on keyboard playing, guided songs, rhythm mode, full screen and MIDI input in supported browsers.

Feedback
