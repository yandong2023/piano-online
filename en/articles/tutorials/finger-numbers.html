Piano Finger Numbers and Basic Fingering

Published: 2025-03-22 · Read time: 12 min · Level: Beginner

Finger numbers

On both hands, thumb is 1 then 2–3–4–5 to the pinky. Memorize quickly so you can follow beginner scores and method books.

Beginner fingering principles

  • Keep a natural curve; play on fingertips
  • Avoid collapsed knuckles and stiff wrists
  • Use finger substitutions to keep phrases smooth
