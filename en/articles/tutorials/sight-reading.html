Sight-Reading Methods

Published: 2025-03-22 · Read time: 16 min · Level: Intermediate

Keep going

Maintain tempo even if you miss a note. Look ahead by a beat or two and prioritize rhythm over perfection.

Daily routine

5–10 minutes a day works better than 1 hour per week. Use easy material and gradually increase difficulty.
