Reading Sheet Music: How to Read Notes

Published: 2025-03-22 · Read time: 20 min · Level: Beginner

Basics

Lines/spaces indicate pitch; treble and bass clefs show hand range. Note heads, stems, and flags combine to show duration.

Count out loud

Practice simple rhythms— quarters, eighths, dotted values— while clapping or tapping before playing.
